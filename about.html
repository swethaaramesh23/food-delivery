Our Story

From a Small Kitchen to the City's Favorite Food Platform

Founded in 2011 in the heart of New York City, Stackly began as a passion project between three culinary school friends who believed great food shouldn't be limited to expensive restaurants. Starting with just 5 restaurant partners and a team of 3 delivery riders, we served our first 100 orders in the first week.

Today, Stackly has grown into a full-scale culinary platform with over 100 restaurant partners, 500+ menu items, and 10,000+ happy customers across the city. We've maintained our founding promise: deliver gourmet-quality food, fast, fresh, and with a smile.

We partner exclusively with farms that practice sustainable agriculture and restaurants that share our values of quality, freshness, and culinary innovation. Every meal delivered is a testament to our commitment to culinary excellence.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Every decision we make is guided by our core purpose and values that have shaped Stackly from day one.

Our Mission

To democratize gourmet dining by making restaurant-quality meals accessible, affordable, and delivered with speed, care, and culinary integrity to every household in the city.


We are committed to supporting local restaurants, creating employment for delivery partners, and providing customers with seamless, joyful ordering experiences every single time.

Our Vision

To become the nation's most trusted and beloved food delivery ecosystem — where chefs, farmers, riders, and customers thrive together in a sustainable, technology-driven culinary community.


By 2030, we envision operating in 50+ cities, partnering with 1,000+ restaurants, and serving 1 million happy customers with zero-emission delivery infrastructure.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2011 — The Beginning

Founded in a Tiny Brooklyn Kitchen

Three friends — Michael, Priya, and Carlos — founded Stackly with a vision to make gourmet delivery possible. First week: 100 orders, 5 partners.

2014 — First Million

Reached 1 Million Orders Milestone

Expanded to 30 restaurant partners and launched the first mobile app with real-time GPS tracking. Hired our 50th employee.

2017 — Going Green

Launched Eco-Friendly Packaging Initiative

Switched to 100% biodegradable containers and partnered with local organic farms. Introduced the Stackly Community Food Fund.

2020 — Tech Evolution

AI-Powered Personalization Launch

Launched our machine learning recommendation engine. Introduced contactless delivery protocols during the pandemic, prioritizing safety.

2024 — Expansion

Multi-City Expansion & 10K+ Customers

Expanded to 3 additional cities. Reached 10,000 active customers, 100 restaurant partners, and launched our loyalty rewards program.

2026 — The Future

Drone & Robot Delivery Pilots

Currently piloting autonomous delivery drones and sidewalk robots in select zones. On track to achieve zero-emission deliveries by 2028.
