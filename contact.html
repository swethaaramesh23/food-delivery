Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery usually take?Add Items
Our average delivery time is 28 minutes. Express orders are prioritized and typically arrive in under 20 minutes. Delivery time may vary based on distance, traffic, and order complexity. You can track your order in real-time via the app or website.
What areas do you deliver to?Add Items
We currently deliver across New York City including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island. We're rapidly expanding to new cities. Enter your zip code on the homepage to check if we deliver to your specific address.
How do I apply coupon codes?Add Items
After adding items to your cart, proceed to checkout. You'll see a "Promo Code" field before the payment step. Enter your coupon code and click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total immediately. One coupon code per order.
Can I modify or cancel my order?Add Items
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 3 minutes of placement, before the restaurant confirms preparation. After confirmation, modifications may not be possible. Contact our support team immediately via live chat or call +1 (800) 555-STACK for urgent cancellations.
How do I become a restaurant partner?Add Items
Email us at partners@stackly.com with your restaurant name, location, and menu. Our partnership team will reach out within 24 hours. The onboarding process takes 3 business days and includes a free professional photoshoot of your dishes.
What happens if my order arrives incorrect or damaged?Add Items
We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If your order is incorrect, missing items, or arrives damaged, contact support within 30 minutes of delivery. We'll either resend the correct order at no charge or provide a full refund — whichever you prefer.
